The Office of the University Registrar

The Office of the University Registrar is responsible for student admissions and registration, grade reporting and transcript of records generation, and maintenance of academic records. The online document request system will help the registrar to manage the file request of the students credentials into the office.

Due to the volume of requests of credentials to the registrar some of the requested file are taking too long to receive and some are even left undone. Also because of the current situation only limited number can enter the campus. The system aims to make requesting credentials much easier and faster .
