How to Process a Request?

Quick Reminder

This option to request for documents is intended ONLY for students/alumni who attended the Sarmiento Campus from Academic Year 2000 to present. For requests falling below the A.Y. 2000, you may request your document by sending an email to the Office of the University Registrar at registrar@bulsu.edu.ph

Please make sure to settle any outstanding balance on your account. You can check your outstanding balance by logging in to the Student Portal or you may contact the Accounting Office at 919-7800 local 1006.

Step 1: Verification

Sign in to your account by entering your Student Number and your E-mail Address.

Step 2: Fill Up the Request Form and Select a Documents

After successful verification, provide your correct information (Student Number, First Name, Last Name etc.).

Note: Please provide your recent contact information and make sure that you are providing a valid email address.

Select the documents you want to request. Also, state the purpose of your request.

Step 3: Review your Request

Review your request for any corrections and modifications needed. Then click submit.

Step 4: Track your Request

After submitting your request, you will be provided by a Reference Number.

Save your Reference Number for later. You can track your request by going to Track your Request Page.
